Frequency and duration

Frequency and duration should be clearly written. Wrong frequency may lead to under-dosing which may result in ineffective treatment. On the other hand, overdosing may lead to side-effects affecting patient safety.

Below are the abbreviations that can be used to indicate the frequency and duration of medication administration.
