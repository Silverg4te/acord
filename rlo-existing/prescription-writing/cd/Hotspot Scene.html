Review Activity

Hotspot Scene Exploration with Early Completion Option

Activity Developer: Aaron Fecowycz

The user can click on the scene to try and identify potential risks. Correctly identifying and selecting a visible risk results in feedback on the specific risk along with the risk item being visually highlighted.

Should the user select an incorrect risk item feedback is provided. If the user incorrectly identifies 5 features of the scene they are offered the opportunity to end the activity and review the learning points.
